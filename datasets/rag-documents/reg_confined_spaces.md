# Industrial Safety Standards: Confined Space Management
Document ID: REG-CSF-12
Version: 1.0
Source: SafeMesh AI Safety Committee

## 1. Scope
These rules apply to all plant areas classified as Confined Spaces, including storage tanks, boiler drums, and pits.

## 2. Atmospheric Entry Standards
- No entry is permitted without an active Confined Space Entry Permit.
- **Oxygen Levels**: Must remain between 19.5% and 23.5%. Entry is prohibited if oxygen levels drop below 19.5%.
- **Toxic Gases**: H2S must remain below 10 ppm. CO must remain below 35 ppm.
- **Continuous Monitoring**: An active watcher must stand outside the space, equipped with continuous remote gas readout.
